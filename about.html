About UK Mortgage Calculator

Helping homebuyers make informed decisions since 2020

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower homebuyers and homeowners with the knowledge and tools they need to make confident financial decisions. We believe that everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, and easy-to-understand information about mortgages, regardless of their financial background.

We are committed to providing a transparent and user-friendly experience, and we are constantly working to improve our calculators and resources to meet the evolving needs of the UK property market.

Why Trust Us

We are committed to providing accurate and unbiased information. Our tools are built on industry-standard formulas and are regularly updated to reflect the latest market trends. We are not affiliated with any specific lender, so you can be sure that our results are impartial.

We also have a dedicated team of researchers who are constantly monitoring the UK mortgage market to ensure that our content is up-to-date and relevant. You can learn more about our editorial process on our editorial policy page.

How We Make Money

We believe in transparency, and that includes being open about how we make money. This website is supported by advertising, which allows us to offer our tools and resources for free.

We also partner with a select group of mortgage brokers who may pay us a referral fee if you choose to work with them. These partnerships do not influence our content, and we only work with brokers who share our commitment to providing a high-quality service.
