ERC-Free Allowances

Most lenders allow penalty-free overpayments up to:

  • 10% per year: Of outstanding balance without ERC
  • Porting: Moving mortgage to new property often ERC-free
  • Death/Illness: ERCs often waived in exceptional circumstances
  • Sale Due to Job Loss: Some lenders waive charges

Understanding Early Repayment Charges

Common ERC Types

  • Percentage: 1-5% of outstanding balance
  • Months Interest: 3-6 months of interest payments
  • Fixed Amount: Set fee regardless of balance
  • Sliding Scale: Reduces each year remaining

When to Consider Paying ERC

  • Significant rate reduction available
  • Long remaining tie-in period
  • Large mortgage balance
  • Break-even within 12-18 months
