Understanding Offset Mortgages

How It Works

  • Your savings balance reduces the mortgage balance for interest calculation
  • You keep instant access to your savings
  • No interest earned on offset savings
  • Typically reduces mortgage term and total interest

Key Benefits

  • Tax-efficient way to use savings
  • Flexible access to funds when needed
  • Can significantly reduce mortgage term
  • Higher rate taxpayers benefit most
