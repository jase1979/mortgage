Editorial Policy

Our commitment to accurate, unbiased, and up-to-date information

Our Principles

At UK Mortgage Calculator, we are committed to providing our users with the highest quality content. Our editorial policy is guided by the following principles:

  • Accuracy: We strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information at all times. Our content is thoroughly researched and fact-checked before publication.
  • Impartiality: We are not affiliated with any specific lender or financial institution. Our content is unbiased and we do not promote any particular products or services.
  • Transparency: We are open and honest about how we make money. We believe that our users have a right to know how our business operates.
  • Clarity: We use clear and concise language to explain complex financial topics. We avoid jargon and technical terms whenever possible.

Our Process

Our content is created by a team of experienced writers and researchers who are experts in the UK mortgage market. Our editorial process includes the following steps:

  1. Research: Our team conducts thorough research to gather the latest information and data from reliable sources.
  2. Writing: Our writers create clear, concise, and engaging content that is easy to understand.
  3. Fact-checking: Our team of fact-checkers verifies the accuracy of all our content before it is published.
  4. Review: Our editors review all content to ensure that it meets our high standards of quality and impartiality.

Corrections

We are committed to correcting any errors in our content as quickly as possible. If you believe that you have found an error, please contact us at admin@mortagagecalculator.co.uk.
